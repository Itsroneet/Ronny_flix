How to Install on iOS

Follow these simple steps to install the app on your iOS device.

  1. Open Safari on your iOS device.
  2. Visit the website where you can download the app.
  3. Tap the "Share" button (the square with an arrow pointing up).
  4. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen".
  5. Tap "Add" in the top-right corner.
  6. Tap the app icon that now appears on your home screen to open the app.
